# Privacy Policy — Battery Reminder

**Last updated:** July 9, 2026
**Developer:** John Cedrick Asad (toyomansi)

## Data Collection
Battery Reminder does not collect, store, or share any personal data. 
The app only reads your device's battery level to display alerts. 
No data leaves your device.

## Permissions
- **FOREGROUND_SERVICE** — keeps battery monitoring active in background
- **RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED** — restarts monitoring after device reboot
- **POST_NOTIFICATIONS** — sends battery level alerts
- **REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS** — prevents Android from stopping the monitoring service
